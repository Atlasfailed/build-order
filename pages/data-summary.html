Games Played Over Time

Distribution of games by date showing when games were played. This distribution is created by two data gathering methods: first looking for recent replays that have a minimum average skill level. Second, adding games from top 100 players on the leaderboard to ensure high skill representation. Games by top 100 players did not have a date restriction except for the map version which is Supreme Isthmus v2.1.

Player Skill Distribution

Distribution of skill ratings across all games. Each player is counted once per game they played. For example, if a player played 10 games, they will be counted 10 times in this distribution. A player's skill rating is recorded at the time of each game. This means a player may appear in multiple skill brackets if their skill changed over time.
